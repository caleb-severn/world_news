World News

Below is a map of the world where if you mouse over each country you get the top news story that day. Each top story has been translated, summarised, and then sentiment analysed (using Vader). The Colours indicate the sentiment,so Red is a Negative sentiment, Blue is neutral, and Green is positive. This means whatever type of news you want to read, or if you want to just expand your horizons, you can. If you click on a country, it will take you the full article that the summary was generated from. A list of website sources can be found on github, if you have suggestions for more reliable sources, email me
